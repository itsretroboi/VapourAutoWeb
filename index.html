Advanced Interior Detail
Standard Vapour Cleaning
$160 – SUV, Van, Hatchback Wagon
$135 – Passenger Car or Truck
Learn More >
Standard interior detail consists of a full interior deep clean using our commercial grade steam machines. We use vapor steam to clean all surfaces inside the vehicle including dash, vents, cup holders, center console, doors, seats and carpets. We also use our high capacity air compressors and vacuums to get in all cracks and crevasses in the vehicle. We also shampoo all carpets in vehicle to try and remove stains and imbedded dirt. Once the vehicle is cleaned, we apply our vinyl and leather protects to all surfaces. This interior detail is based off of a time limit of 2 hours for cars/trucks and 2.5 hours for SUV/VAN/Hatchback/Wagon. This detail is our basic detail and perfection is not expected with this package. If you are looking for something with more attention to detail and near perfection, please see our immaculate detail. Minor touch-ups will be done, and door jams and headliners will not be serviced on this detail.
Please remember some stains and surfaces are damaged and will not be returned to previous condition no matter how many times it is cleaned. A technician will show you these spots if any during pick up.
