Standard Interior Detail
Standard Vapour Cleaning

$135 – Passenger Car or Truck
Learn More >
Standard interior detail consists of a full interior deep clean using our idustrial grade steam machines. We use vapour steam to clean all surfaces inside the vehicle including dash, vents, cup holders, center console, doors, seats and carpets. We also use our high capacity vacuums to get in all cracks and crevasses in the vehicle. We also steam all of the carpets in vehicle to try and remove stains and imbedded dirt as well as salt. Shampooing and deep dirt removal can be added as an additional service. Once the vehicle is cleaned, we apply our vinyl and leather protects to all surfaces. This interior detail is based off of a time limit of 2 hours for cars/trucks and 2.5 hours for SUV/VAN/Hatchback/Wagon. This detail is our basic detail and perfection is not expected with this package. If you are looking for something with more attention to detail and near perfection, please see our showroom detail. Door jambs and headliners will not be serviced on this detail.
Please remember some stains and surfaces are damaged and will not be returned to previous condition no matter how many times it is cleaned. A technician will show you these spots if any during pick up.
