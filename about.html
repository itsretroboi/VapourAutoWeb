Our short story

We deliver the cleanest.

Vapourauto is the region’s only vapour based detailing shop for premium car detailing. We may not be the cheapest of the bunch but stand firmly behind the fact that no one in Ottawa does it like us! From vapour to our high end detailing products we are crazy about what we do.

Quality over quantity is our motto.

Having 8+ years of detailing experience our business offers a level of quality that is usually overlooked at other other shops. Our detailers are trained to very high standards to ensure the best possible outcome for the client. We are not your typical "car wash" but rather a group of automotive enthusiasts that want to ensure the best possible fit and finish for your ride.

We do not worry about the number of cars we detail on a weekly or monthly basis, in fact, we are just concerned about how satisfied customers are. We understand that our business is only going to thrive with loyal customers like yourself. With this in mind, we constantly provide the best service and detailing to your area that no other local business can provide. The most important thing is to ensure every customer is happy with the results prior to taking deilvery of their vehicle. We are driven by results and by customer satisfaction.

At Vapourauto, the customer always comes first. Whether it is a simple salt removal or in depth showroom package our team will ensure the highest level of quality for your vehicle.
